Chad

Photographer. Creator. Explorer.

Hi, I’m Chad—a photographer with a passion for capturing moments and telling stories through images. Originally from Whitehall, PA, I found my love for art in Philadelphia, where I explored galleries, co-hosted art shows, and started my journey behind the lens. My work has taken me across the U.S., discovering inspiration in museums, cities, and everyday life. Today, I call Brooklyn home, continuing to create, host art events, and chase new perspectives wherever I go.

Art Show Co-Host

Portrait Studio Owner

Founded and managed a portrait studio, focusing on capturing unique and meaningful moments for clients.
